Horizon Inventory Management - Advanced Adequacy in Stock Levels

Horizon Inventory Management, founded by Gutsaga Technology, is a paradigm shift from traditional forecasting to a dynamic, AI-enhanced approach to inventory control. Utilizing the Theory of Magnetism and AI, Horizon ensures your inventory levels are perpetually optimized.

How It Works:

Each day, Horizon processes your latest inventory data. Our sophisticated engines analyze stock levels for adequacy, utilizing Magnetism Theory and AI to provide immediate recommendations for replenishment, thus avoiding the pitfalls of forecast-based systems.

Benefits:
  • Real-time Inventory Management: Horizon's daily adjustments ensure optimal stock levels, contributing to at least 20% less inventory than traditional forecasting systems.
  • Enhanced Automation: Reduce the hours spent on inventory management, allowing your employees to focus on other critical tasks.
  • Improved Financial Outcomes: Experience better inventory turnover and return on investment, coupled with reduced lost sales.
Interesting Case Study:

A pharmaceutical chain with more than 350 pharmacies automated their ordering process using Horizon for 9 months, completely hands-off. Without any human intervention, the results were remarkable, showcasing the efficiency and effectiveness of Horizon's AI-driven inventory management.

Redistribution - Automated Stock Balancing

Redistribution is our automated system designed to optimize inventory across various store locations, reducing overstock and preventing product wastage through intelligent product reallocation.

How It Works:

The system automatically identifies products that are overstocked, nearing expiration, or experiencing low sales at one location and reallocates them to stores where they are in higher demand. This process includes handling store closures and department changes.

Benefits:
  • Enhanced Inventory Turnover: Improve your inventory turnover rates and financial performance.
  • Reduced Waste: Minimize the need to discount or dispose of overstock and near-expiry products.
  • Efficient Management: Simplify the process of managing inventory during store closures or department shifts.
  • Increased Sales Opportunities: Maximize sales potential by ensuring high-demand products are available where needed.
Interesting Case Study:

After a 2-week pilot, a pharmacy chain of approximately 120 pharmacies faced a sudden change in their supplier's payment terms. Required to prepay for stock, they leveraged Redistribution for efficient stock management between pharmacies. Within just two weeks, the system automatically balanced their stock, leading to significant financial savings and enabling safe prepayment for new stock purchases.

Outlet - Advanced Inventory Clearance

Outlet by Gutsaga Technology is a cutting-edge solution designed to automatically manage and reduce inventory levels for specific SKUs across the entire retail network, including warehouses and points of sale (POS), by a target date.

How It Works:

Outlet automatically adjusts product prices based on price elasticity and risk management, and redistributes SKUs between POSs to minimize logistics costs, ensuring efficient clearance of inventory by the specified target date.

Benefits:
  • Time-Saving for Category Managers: Reduces the workload of managing sell-out products, traditionally taking up 20%-40% of their time.
  • Increased Revenue: Enhances the potential to earn more by selling products more efficiently before deadlines.
  • Shelf Management: Ensures timely clearance of old products to make space for new inventory.
Interesting Case Study:

A client with about 120 cosmetic stores used Outlet to manage 5-8k products in their sell-out section. The system handled price changes and redistribution automatically, requiring only one person to spend 15 minutes daily overseeing the process. Previously, 12 category managers would each spend 20-30 minutes daily managing these SKUs.

Assortment - Strategic Product Placement and Optimization

The Assortment subproduct from ISB is designed to strategically manage product placements in retail. It focuses on spreading high-ranking products across Points of Sale (POS) and removing long-term unsold items, optimizing the retail space efficiently.

How It Works:

Utilizing a sophisticated system, Assortment calculates rankings based on sales units, volume, and margin for each SKU across POS. It identifies missing high-ranking products in POS assortments and suggests their addition. Simultaneously, it identifies products with extended no-sale periods and recommends their removal.

Benefits:
  • Increased Sales: Ensuring high-demand products are available at the right POS.
  • Improved Customer Satisfaction: Offering a more tailored product range to meet consumer demands.
  • Better Inventory Management: Enhancing ITR and ROI by eliminating stagnant stock.
