Second visit to Armenia

May 17-21, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

Continued relationships with Armenian pharmaceutical market players, carried out earlier initiated projects, and discussed future actions:

  • May 18th: Met with PHESA association, discussed sales possibilities to small pharmacies.
  • May 19th: Presented diagnostics results to "Alfa Pharm" and agreed on starting a pilot project.
  • May 20th: Met with DV GROUP LLC's IT manager David Tsirunyan to discuss the pilot project.
  • May 19th: Re-met with "Asteria" network, owner attended.
  • May 20th: Met with individual company owner Yevgeniya Sargsyan, agreement on sales and servicing functions in Armenia.
Benefits

Began a new pilot project, strengthened relationships with key pharmaceutical actors, forged new contacts. Also, conditions were created for selling cluster solutions to small pharmacies through a local sales and service partner.

Visit to Mongolia

March 12-16, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

The mission aimed to visit Monos group companies (13 firms) and independent pharmacy networks, specifically Monos Pharma Trade, which has a platform involving nearly all Mongolian pharmacies. Two Monos group representatives, Bayasgalan Tamir and his assistant Knonova Ariuntuul, accompanied us:

  • March 14th: A significant presentation for the Monos group involving around 30 leaders, showcasing the cluster and members' solutions.
  • March 14th: Signed a cooperation agreement with Monos Pharma Trade.
  • March 15th: Attended an event for independent pharmacies organized by Monos Pharma Trade.
  • March 15th: Meeting arranged with "Chumet" and "Ento" pharmacy network's top management.
Mission Benefits:

The mission advanced the introduction of cluster members' solutions and fostered relationships with leaders in Mongolia's pharmaceutical market. Additionally, Monos group representatives are expected to visit Lithuania in summer 2023 to acquaint themselves more deeply with the solutions implemented in the Lithuanian market.

Meeting with Armenia's PHESA

February 8-9, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

We engaged with Armenia's PHESA association to establish new opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry. Activities included:

  • Signing agreements with key pharmaceutical players
  • Planning pilot projects with "Alfa Pharm"
  • Conducting diagnostics with "Asteria" and "Tsiranmarket" pharmacy networks

Additional collaborations and plans were discussed to expand our reach in the region.

Benefits of the Mission:

The mission's major accomplishment was securing two pilot projects, facilitating valuable connections and promising collaborations within the Armenian pharmaceutical market.

Visit to Uzbekistan - "Eurasian Pharmaceutical Summit 2022"

October 10-14, 2022
Activities and Objectives:

The mission's purpose was to participate in the "Eurasian Pharmaceutical Summit 2022," the largest regional conference with participation from not only Asian but also Caucasian, Moldovan, and Ukrainian companies. Opportunities to meet the Monos group owner and many other companies at the conference were provided. A 20-minute presentation was also delivered.

Mission Benefits:

Enabled exposure to a broad spectrum of Asian region pharmaceutical representatives, expanded the partner network, gained insights into market trends, and received industry feedback for product refinement.

Collaboration with Estonian Clusters

September 19-21, 2022
Activities and Objectives:

The mission involved visits to two clusters: the Estonian ICT cluster, represented by Doris Pold, and the "Connected Health Cluster," part of the "Tehnopol Foundation," represented by Martin Goroško.

  • Met with Estonian ICT cluster representatives and signed a cooperation agreement. Members presented solutions for the pharmaceutical market.
  • Met with "Connected Health Cluster" representatives and signed a cooperation agreement. This cluster represents the Estonian pharmaceutical market.
Mission Benefits:

Enabled connections with Estonian IT and health tech clusters, secured collaboration agreements, and provided new opportunities to enter the Estonian market.
