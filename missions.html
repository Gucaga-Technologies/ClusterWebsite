2 Visits from Mongolia to Lithuania

August 17-22, August 26-30, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

The primary goal was for the Monos Group to understand the solutions that Cluster members have implemented in Lithuania. Key activities during the visit included:

  • Meetings with representatives from "Pharmams" and "Inherent Simplicity Baltic".
  • Inspection of the warehouse and inventory management systems at Lithuanian pharmacy chains.
  • Meeting with representatives from "Equinox" and "Optas".
  • A demonstration of the Singleton lockers.
Benefits:

The interactions facilitated a deeper understanding of the systems in place, paving the way for new project initiatives and strengthening partnerships with key stakeholders.

  • Launch of a pilot project related to the inventory management of "Inherent Simplicity Baltic".
  • The Monos Group expressed interest in obtaining consultancy from "Equinox" regarding warehouse management.
  • An agreement with Singleton to evaluate specialized lockers.

Second Visit to Armenia

May 17-21, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

The mission focused on continuing and strengthening relationships with Armenian pharmaceutical market players, implementing previously initiated projects, and planning future collaboration. Key activities included:

  • Meeting with the PHESA association to explore sales opportunities to small pharmacies.
  • Presentation of diagnostic results to "Alfa Pharm," leading to an agreement on a pilot project.
  • Discussions with DV GROUP LLC regarding the upcoming pilot project.
  • A follow-up meeting with the "Asteria" network, with the owner in attendance.
  • An agreement with individual company on sales and servicing functions in Armenia.
Benefits:

The visit led to the initiation of a new pilot project, the fortification of existing relationships, and the creation of new contacts within the Armenian pharmaceutical sector. Additionally, conditions were established for selling cluster solutions to small pharmacies through local partnerships.

Visit to Mongolia

March 12-16, 2023
Activities and Objectives:

The mission aimed to visit Monos group companies (13 firms) and independent pharmacy networks, specifically Monos Pharma Trade, which has a platform involving nearly all Mongolian pharmacies. Two Monos group representatives, Bayasgalan Tamir and his assistant Knonova Ariuntuul, accompanied us. Activities included:

  • A significant presentation for the Monos group, showcasing the cluster and members' solutions.
  • Signing a cooperation agreement with Monos Pharma Trade.
  • Attending an event for independent pharmacies organized by Monos Pharma Trade.
  • Meeting arranged with "Chumet" and "Ento" pharmacy network's top management.
Mission Benefits:

The mission advanced the introduction of cluster members' solutions and fostered relationships with leaders in Mongolia's pharmaceutical market. Additionally, Monos group representatives are expected to visit Lithuania in summer 2023 to acquaint themselves more deeply with the solutions implemented in the Lithuanian market.

Presentation at Uzbekistan - "Eurasian Pharmaceutical Summit 2022"

Collaboration with Estonian Clusters

September 19-21, 2022
Activities and Objectives:

The mission involved visits to two clusters: the Estonian ICT cluster, and the "Connected Health Cluster," part of the "Tehnopol Foundation,".

  • Met with Estonian ICT cluster representatives and signed a cooperation agreement. Members presented solutions for the pharmaceutical market.
  • Met with "Connected Health Cluster" representatives and signed a cooperation agreement. This cluster represents the Estonian pharmaceutical market.
Mission Benefits:

Enabled connections with Estonian IT and health tech clusters, secured collaboration agreements, and provided new opportunities to enter the Estonian market.
