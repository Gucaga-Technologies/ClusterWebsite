Our Missions

Mission to Mongolia (March 12-16, 2023)

Members visited Mongolia to engage with companies in the Monos group and independent pharmacies. Activities included presentations, signing agreements, and meetings with pharmacy networks. The mission expanded the solution outreach in Mongolia's pharmaceutical market.

Second Visit to Armenia (May 17-21, 2023)

The PharmaIT cluster continued engagement in Armenia, including meetings with associations, initiating pilot projects, and discussions for further cooperation. This mission strengthened ties with key pharmaceutical actors in Armenia.

Mission to Armenia (Feb 7-10, 2023)

The mission aimed at meeting significant actors in Armenia's pharmaceutical market, leading to diagnostic agreements, pilot projects, and collaborations with key pharmacy networks.

Mission to Uzbekistan (October 10-14, 2022)

The mission focused on participation in the "Eurasian Pharmaceutical Summit 2022," networking with companies, and presenting insights to contribute to understanding regional pharmaceutical trends.

Mission to Estonia (September 19-21, 2022)

Participants engaged in strategic meetings with Estonian clusters, signing cooperation agreements, and establishing relationships with IT companies for solutions within the PharmaIT context.
