Havlík Apoteka

Welcome to Havlík Apoteka

Havlík Apoteka is a Czech organic skincare love brand, established in 2014 by Zuzana Krejčí inspired by a boundless love for nature and enthusiasm for pure beauty. Havlík Apoteka represents a fusion of modern science, organic ingredients, uncompromising quality, time-tested recipes and the ancient history of herbs that have known healing effects since ancient times. The proven recipes are supplemented with patented active substances that are verified by clinical studies

Why Choose Havlík Apoteka?

  • Our brand has:
  • Organic certified skin care, body care, hair care, intimate care, Child, woman and man care products with the purest ingredients.
  • Czech Hand Made
  • Ancient recipes proven with time (combining storytelling, tradition and modern science)
  • Love brand – we actively promote self-love and self-care education through all the concept and materials (like affirmations) we give in our products.
  • Unique Marketing - We open taboo topics. The central theme is self- love. In the era of digitization, we publish unique newspapers in many world languages that are popular with customers and have won prestigious awards for them. We create a unique omnichannel business space that no one else offers and because of that, we have fantastic customer retention. Women especially, love our products!
