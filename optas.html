Welcome to OPTAS

In pharmacy companies more than half of the people work on irregular shifts (in retail, logistics, etc.). Usually, schedules are managed with non-specialized tools like Excel. A manager with 50+ employees can spend up to 20+ hours per week for scheduling. Managers having to deal with such time-consuming tasks can often forget to check-in with employees leaving them feeling unvalued or unheard. Modern automated scheduling software can make this nightmare into a joyful adventure. OPTAS AI based algorithms make complex schedules faster, more efficient and with employee availability in mind. Managers are freed from scheduling burden and can dedicate more time to employees.

Why Choose OPTAS?

  • Innovative Technology: OPTAS employs AI algorithms to create complex schedules swiftly, keeping in mind labor laws and employee availability.
  • Expertise: With proven success in large Lithuanian companies, OPTAS ensures that scheduling is not only easy but also compliant and employee-centric.
  • Efficient Workforce Management: Empowering managers to focus more on employees rather than administrative tasks, OPTAS significantly enhances workplace satisfaction.

OPTAS Automated Scheduling - AI-Driven Workforce Management

OPTAS is an AI-based automated scheduling tool that revolutionizes workforce management, ensuring effective schedule creation and management.

How It Works:
  • AI-powered automatic scheduling to optimize shift allocations.
  • Ensures schedules comply with labor laws.
  • Employee input is considered for availability and preferred work times.
  • Automated notifications for new schedules and related updates.
  • Tracks actual working hours and provides analytical data.
  • Seamless integration with payroll and access control systems.
Benefits:

OPTAS system empowers managers to create complex and Labour Law compliant employee schedules easy and quickly. OPTAS helps to save not only money and time, but it also makes employees more satisfied with their workplace.

OPTAS is a good match for pharmacy companies, especially for retail and warehouse personnel management.
