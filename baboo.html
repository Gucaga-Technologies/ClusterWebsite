Welcome to Baboo

Baboo is a UK-based brand, established in 2016 by product experts who worked with baby accessory products for over 20 years, offers a diverse range of baby and mother products that combine style, quality, and functionality.

Why Choose Baboo?

  • Marketing material: We offer wide image gallery ready for e-commerce, lifestyle images and videos.
  • PIM: Whole product data can be accessed and downloaded within few clicks in our product information management (PIM) system.
  • Order preparation: Due to our bonded warehouse, the order preparation can be finished in 2 days.
  • Local market needs: Local distributor’s feedback is considered when developing new Baboo products.
