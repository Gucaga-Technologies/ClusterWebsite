Warehouse Management System (WMS) - Equinox Europe

Equinox Europe's VISION WMS is an elite solution with over 28 years of continuous evolution, designed to master every facet of warehouse operations. It elevates accuracy and throughput within the same operational space, enhancing service levels across e-commerce, distribution, 3PL, and manufacturing sectors.

How It Works:

VISION WMS integrates with your warehouse's existing systems to manage processes from receiving to shipping. It provides precise inventory tracking, order accuracy, and streamlined operations, supported by value-added services to maximize warehouse potential.

Benefits:
  • Proven Efficiency: Decades of refinement ensure that VISION WMS optimizes your warehouse's operational flow.
  • Scalable Accuracy: Tailor-made to support growth, VISION manages increased volumes without sacrificing precision.
  • Strategic Insight: With VISION, gain actionable data that empowers better inventory and process decisions.
Equinox Automated Solutions - Precision in Motion

For Pharmaceutical industry clients the company makes automated solutions for order picking/packing to sorting to delivery destinations. Also solutions for receiving goods to warehouse, measuring them if needed and moving them with conveyors to storage zones.

How It Works:
  • Conveyor Systems: Our advanced conveyor belts move goods efficiently through various stages of the warehouse, from unloading to storage and dispatch.
  • Automated Sorting Systems: High-speed sorting systems that accurately organize products based on destination, size, and weight, optimizing the delivery process.
  • AS/RS Cranes and Shuttles: Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) that enhance storage density and retrieval speed, minimizing storage space and time.
  • Robots, AGV/AMR: Our use of robots and Automated Guided Vehicles (AGV) / Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR) provides flexible, scalable automation solutions for varied operational needs.
Benefits:
  • Increased Efficiency: Our automated processes drastically reduce the time and labor involved in warehouse operations, directly contributing to heightened operational efficiency.
  • Enhanced Accuracy: By minimizing manual handling errors, we ensure higher accuracy in order fulfillment, enhancing customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Cost Savings: Operational optimization leads to significant savings, reducing overheads and enhancing the overall profitability of pharmaceutical logistics.
